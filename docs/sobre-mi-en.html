About Me

I'm Tori, an indie game developer and Spanish-speaking content creator.
I create my own games from scratch using the Unity game engine. I also make videos for my YouTube channel where I share the development process and do challenges like "making a game in 7 days".

Bio

I was born in Chiclana de la Frontera (Cádiz), on May 3rd, 1999.
I currently live in Madrid with Silvia.
Since I was a child, I’ve always been interested in technology and I remember countless afternoons watching my dad play on the Spectrum or the PS2.
My first experience with video games was probably with some old PC game. My first console was a Nintendo DS with Animal Crossing.
